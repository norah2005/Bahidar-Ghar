ABOUT US

Welcome to The Grand Bahidar Hotel, where luxury meets comfort in the heart of Bandipur. Established in 1925 by Amelia Bennett, our historic hotel blends timeless elegance with modern amenities, offering guests a unique experience marked by personalized service and eco-friendly practices. Located near vibrant local attractions, we pride ourselves on our role within the community, supporting local businesses and sustainability initiatives. At The Bahidar Hotel, you're not just a guest – you're family, and we look forward to making your stay truly special. Book your stay today and discover the magic of The Bahidar Hotel.
